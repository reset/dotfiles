# How to Download & Watch TV Shows 🎬

Hey Wonder! This guide walks you through how to download any TV show you want and watch it on Plex through the Apple TV. Once it's set up it's pretty simple — you pick the show, it downloads automatically, and it shows up in Plex when it's ready.

> **One thing to know first:** You need to be on our home WiFi for this to work. It won't work from outside the house.

> **⚖️ A very important legal notice:** We only use this to download shows we already own on DVD, Blu-ray, or some other format we definitely have *somewhere*. This is purely for personal backup purposes, as is our legal right. We would never dream of obtaining content we hadn't already paid for. Never ever. 😇

---

## Step 1 — Find and request your show

Open a browser on any device at home and go to:

### **[http://sonarr.home](http://sonarr.home)**

This is our TV show manager. When you add a show here it automatically finds it, downloads it, and adds it to Plex.

1. Click **+ Add New** in the left sidebar (or the big **+** button)
2. Type the name of the show you want in the search box
3. Find your show in the results and click on it

---

## Step 2 — Configure the show

A settings panel will pop up. Most of it you can leave as-is, but here's what to pay attention to:

**Monitor** — this controls which episodes get downloaded:
- **All Episodes** — downloads everything, including old seasons and new ones as they air
- **Future Episodes** — only downloads new episodes going forward (good if you don't care about catching up)
- **Latest Season** — downloads the current season only

**Quality Profile** — leave this set to **Any**. It picks a good quality automatically.

**Root Folder** — leave this as `/downloads/tv-shows`

When you're happy, click the big blue **Add + Search** button at the bottom. That's it! Sonarr will start finding and downloading it automatically.

---

## Step 3 — Check on the download

You can watch the progress from the **Activity** tab in the left sidebar. You'll see the show queued or actively downloading there.

How long it takes depends on the show:
- A single episode: usually **5–20 minutes**
- A full season: could be **a few hours**
- Multiple seasons: could be **overnight**

You don't need to leave the page open — it keeps downloading in the background.

---

## Step 4 — Watch it on Plex

Once the download finishes, it shows up in Plex automatically (usually within a minute or two).

On the **Apple TV**:
1. Open the **Plex** app
2. Go to **TV Shows** in your library
3. Find your show — it'll be there!

If it's not showing up after the download finished, you can nudge Plex to refresh by going to the show in Plex on the Apple TV and pressing the touchpad to get the options menu, then tapping **Fix Incorrect Match** or just waiting another minute.

---

## Quick tips

**Monitoring new episodes as they air** — if you added a show with "All Episodes" or "Future Episodes", Sonarr will automatically download new episodes when they come out. You don't need to do anything.

**Want to see what's already available?** — Everything that's been downloaded is already in Plex. Just browse the TV Shows library.

**Show not finding anything?** — Some shows are harder to find than others. Let Jamie know and he can troubleshoot.

**Removing a show** — If you want to stop monitoring a show (so it stops downloading new episodes), find it in Sonarr and click the trash icon. You can choose to keep the files or delete them.

## The short version

1. Go to **http://sonarr.home** on home WiFi
2. Click **+**, search for your show, click it
3. Pick **All Episodes**, click **Add + Search**
4. Wait for it to download, then find it in **Plex** on the Apple TV ✅
